Banana Brothers

Banana Brothers is a group of highly sophisticated monkey fans and banana lovers.
The group itself then devides into the outer circle (The Deep Jungle) and the inner circle (Council of Monkeys).
Members of the council are the ones that contribute the most to the already rich and vast culture of The Banana Brothers.
Due to their activity and contribution to the monkey community they have more prestige, rights and say in the matter of things.
Banana Brothers induldge in many ways of self-expression such as artwork, gaming, scientific discussions, pihlosophising and obviously
sharing the finest quality of memes.If you're interested in becoming a fine gentlemonkey (or lady) yourself, be sure to pay us a visit!
